**A data-driven case study building a predictive model to optimize a financial services marketing funnel.**

## The Challenge
Inefficient lead prioritization was wasting marketing spend with a baseline conversion rate of only **11.3%**.

## Key Insights
- **Macro-Economic Drivers:** External factors impact conversion **3.8x more** than customer demographics.
- **High-Precision Scoring:** Identified a lead tier with **77% precision**, enabling **6x higher conversion rates**.
- **Engagement Threshold:** The largest funnel drop-off (72.7%) occurs before the 5-minute call mark.
